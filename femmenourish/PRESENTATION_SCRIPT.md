## üéØ Key Delivery Tips

### Voice & Body Language:
- **Volume**: Loud enough for back row to hear
- **Pace**: Slightly slower than normal conversation (judges need to process)
- **Pauses**: After hook, after showing diagnosis, before close
- **Eye Contact**: Scan all judges, don't fixate on one
- **Hands**: Use gestures when saying "116 million women" (show scale)
- **Energy**: High energy but professional, not frantic

### What to Show on Screen:
1. Homepage (5 sec)
2. Assessment tab with filled form (10 sec)
3. Diagnosis results (10 sec)
4. Pune meal plan (15 sec)
5. London meal plan (15 sec)
6. Shopping list + PDF (10 sec)
7. Optional: cities.json for scalability proof (5 sec)

### Common Demo Pitfalls to Avoid:
- ‚ùå Apologizing ("Sorry this is slow...")
- ‚ùå Explaining technical details ("This uses Streamlit...")
- ‚ùå Reading from slides
- ‚ùå Saying "um" or "so basically"
- ‚ùå Going over time (judges will cut you off)
- ‚ùå Clicking aimlessly while talking

### If Something Breaks:
- **API fails**: "While that loads, let me show you the PDF we generated earlier‚Äî"
- **Internet down**: Switch to backup video immediately
- **Wrong click**: "Let me show you the other feature‚Äî" (don't acknowledge mistake)
- **Blank screen**: Keep talking, describe what SHOULD be showing
